# Data Card: Campus Chat Synthetic Data

## Purpose
The data supports a classroom demonstration of intent classification, document validation, retrieval, evaluation, deployment manifests, and evidence traces.

## Files
- `campus_intents.csv`: synthetic utterances labeled with eight intents.
- `campus_documents.csv`: fictional campus support and policy documents.
- `campus_benchmark.csv`: expected intent and expected source document for evaluation questions.
- `sample_feedback.csv`: example post-deployment feedback requiring review before reuse.

## Limitations
The data is small, English-only, synthetic, and intentionally simplified. It does not represent actual university policy, accessibility needs, multilingual use, demographic variation, or the full range of student questions.

## Permitted use
Teaching, experimentation, testing, and project scaffolding.

## Prohibited interpretation
Do not treat any content as official policy or as evidence that the model is suitable for high-stakes advising.
